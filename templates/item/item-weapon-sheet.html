Damage System Notes:

Damage Tests: Roll 3d10 vs Damage Rating

  • Each die ≤ Damage Rating = 1 success
  • 1 success = Minor wound
  • 2 successes = Major wound
  • 3+ successes = Mortal wound

Target Number Modifiers: Target's Physique and armor reduce the effective Damage Rating.
